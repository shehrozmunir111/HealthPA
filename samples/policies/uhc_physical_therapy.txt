UnitedHealthcare Medical Policy — Outpatient Physical Therapy & Low Back Pain

Diagnosis coding (ICD-10-CM):
- Low back pain, unspecified is M54.50.
- Vertebrogenic low back pain is M54.51.
- Other low back pain is M54.59.
- Lumbago with sciatica, unspecified side, is M54.40.

Physical therapy procedure coding (CPT):
- Physical therapy evaluation, low complexity, is CPT 97161.
- Physical therapy evaluation, moderate complexity, is CPT 97162.
- Physical therapy evaluation, high complexity, is CPT 97163.
- Therapeutic exercise (each 15 minutes) is CPT 97110.
- Manual therapy techniques (each 15 minutes) is CPT 97140.

Authorization criteria: outpatient physical therapy for low back pain is covered
when conservative management is documented and a physical therapy evaluation
establishes medical necessity. An initial PT evaluation (97161-97163) does not
require prior authorization; ongoing therapeutic exercise (97110) is covered per
the established plan of care.
