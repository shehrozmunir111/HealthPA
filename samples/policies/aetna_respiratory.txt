Aetna Prior Authorization Policy — Lower Respiratory Infections

Coverage and coding guidance:
- Pneumonia, unspecified organism is coded J18.9 under ICD-10-CM.
- Bacterial pneumonia, not elsewhere classified, is J15.9.
- Acute bronchitis, unspecified, is J20.9.

Imaging:
- A single-view chest x-ray is CPT 71045.
- A two-view (frontal and lateral) chest x-ray is CPT 71046.
- Chest CT without contrast is CPT 71250.

Medical necessity: imaging must be supported by documented signs/symptoms
(e.g., productive cough, fever, abnormal lung exam). Prior authorization is not
required for a standard two-view chest x-ray when pneumonia is suspected.
