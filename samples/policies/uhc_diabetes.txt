UnitedHealthcare Medical Policy — Diabetes Management & Monitoring

Diagnosis coding (ICD-10-CM):
- Type 2 diabetes mellitus without complications is E11.9.
- Type 2 diabetes mellitus with hyperglycemia is E11.65.
- Type 1 diabetes mellitus without complications is E10.9.

Laboratory and monitoring (CPT):
- Hemoglobin A1c (glycated hemoglobin) testing is CPT 83036.
- Glucose, quantitative, blood, is CPT 82947.
- Comprehensive metabolic panel is CPT 80053.

Authorization criteria: routine HbA1c monitoring (83036) for an established
diabetes diagnosis is covered without prior authorization, typically up to four
times per year. Document the diabetes diagnosis and the monitoring plan.
