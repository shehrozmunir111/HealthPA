Cigna Medical Coverage Policy — Musculoskeletal Imaging

Knee:
- Primary osteoarthritis of the right knee is ICD-10-CM M17.11; left knee M17.12;
  bilateral is M17.0.
- MRI of the knee without contrast is CPT 73721; with contrast is CPT 73722.
- Knee x-ray, 1-2 views, is CPT 73560.

Authorization criteria for knee MRI: failed conservative therapy (>= 6 weeks)
OR mechanical symptoms (locking, instability) OR suspicion of internal
derangement. Document prior treatment and exam findings.
